Methodology

  • Implied Growth = (Trailing PE / Forward PE) - 1, capped at ±50%
  • Target 1Y = CMP × (1 + implied growth) — earnings grow, PE stays constant
  • Target 2Y = CMP × (1 + 70% of growth)² — discounted 30% for compounding uncertainty
  • Target 3Y = CMP × (1 + 70% of growth)³ — further discounted
  • Analyst Tgt = Consensus 12-month price target from sell-side analysts (Yahoo Finance)
  • Return % = Price change from Jan 4, 2021 (first trading day) to today
  • Mkt Cap = Current market capitalization from Yahoo Finance

AI Revenue Exposure Rating

  • High AI — Core AI business: AI chips, AI platforms, AI infrastructure, AI-native products (>30% revenue tied to AI)
  • Mod AI — AI enhances products/margins; meaningful but not primary growth driver
  • Neutral — Minimal direct AI revenue impact; may use AI operationally
  • Low AI — Traditional business; AI adoption is incremental at best
  • AI Risk — Business model at risk of AI disruption

Interest Rate Sensitivity

  • Benefits — Earns float income or benefits from rate environment
  • Mild + — Defensive/dividend stocks that benefit from rate cuts
  • Neutral — Balanced exposure; moderate impact from rate changes
  • Mild - — Growth premiums compress with higher rates
  • Sensitive — Extreme valuations; very high duration risk from rate hikes

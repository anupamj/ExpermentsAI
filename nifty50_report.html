Methodology

  • Implied Growth = (Trailing PE / Forward PE) - 1, capped at ±50%
  • Target 1Y = CMP × (1 + implied growth) — earnings grow, PE stays constant
  • Target 2Y = CMP × (1 + 70% of growth)² — discounted 30% for compounding uncertainty
  • Target 3Y = CMP × (1 + 70% of growth)³ — further discounted
  • Analyst Tgt = Consensus 12-month price target from sell-side analysts (Yahoo Finance)
  • Return % = Price change from Jan 4, 2021 (first trading day) to today

Crude Oil Sensitivity Rating

  • Strong + — Direct beneficiary of rising crude (upstream producers, domestic energy substitutes)
  • Mild + — Indirect beneficiary (refining margins, energy alternatives)
  • Neutral — Minimal or no direct crude price link (IT, banking, pharma, insurance)
  • Mild - — Input costs or demand mildly impacted (FMCG, cement, autos, delivery)
  • Strong - — Heavily impacted (paint cos with crude-derived inputs, OMCs, carmakers)
